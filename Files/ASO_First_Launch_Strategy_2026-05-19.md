# ASO First Launch Strategy

> Superseded: current app name and first-launch metadata are now defined in [ASO_SideProfit_Metadata_2026-05-21.md](ASO_SideProfit_Metadata_2026-05-21.md).

Research date: 2026-05-19

Product working name: DevCal / IndieProfit

## 繁體中文摘要

這個 App 第一次上架時，不建議把主定位放在「記帳」、「預算管理」或「一般支出追蹤」。那些關鍵字雖然搜尋量高，但競爭非常激烈，而且成熟競品已經有大量評分、評論與下載歷史。

更好的首發定位是：

> 給獨立開發者、SaaS builder、side project 創作者使用的專案收益、成本與回本追蹤工具。

建議首發名稱與副標題：

| 欄位 | English | 繁體中文理解 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| App Name | `IndieProfit` | 獨立開發者的獲利追蹤 |
| Subtitle | `App Revenue & Break-even` | App 收入與回本追蹤 |
| Core promise | Track whether your side project is becoming profitable. | 看清楚你的 side project 是否真的正在變成一門生意。 |

核心策略：

- 用 `IndieProfit` 做品牌，讓目標使用者一眼知道這是給 indie builder 的工具。
- 用 `App Revenue & Break-even` 承接搜尋意圖，抓住 app revenue、revenue tracker、break-even 這類高意圖關鍵字。
- 不在標題或副標題主打「budget」或「記帳」，避免被使用者誤會成一般個人財務 App。
- 在 hidden keyword field 和 description 裡放入 `expense`、`income`、`tracker`、`startup`、`project`、`saas`、`side hustle` 等輔助詞。

## Product ASO Positioning

The strongest first-launch position is not generic budgeting or personal finance.

Recommended position:

> A profit and break-even tracker for indie developers building apps, SaaS, and side projects.

Why this is better:

- Generic expense tracker keywords are crowded and dominated by apps with thousands of ratings.
- Indie revenue tracker apps are more focused, but many are integration-first, Mac-only, or revenue-only.
- This product can own the earlier workflow: manual project revenue, developer expenses, time cost, and break-even progress before Stripe, RevenueCat, App Store Connect, or AdMob dashboards are worth connecting.

## Competitor ASO Patterns

### Closest Niche Competitors

| App | Current title pattern | Subtitle / visible promise | ASO lesson |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Vertex - Revenue Tracker | Brand + keyword | Track Your Business Income | Clear business revenue language, project pipeline framing, no-account privacy angle. |
| IndieBar - Revenue Tracker | Niche brand + keyword | Stripe & RevenueCat Menu Bar | Extremely clear indie developer audience and integration keywords. |
| ADWize: AD Revenue Tracker | Brand + specific revenue keyword | Real-time Ad Revenue Tracker | Repeats the highest-intent keyword naturally across title and subtitle. |
| RevenueTrack | Brand only | Store revenue tracker | Short brand, subtitle does the keyword work. Strong for App Store sales intent. |
| MRR Tracker | Exact-match keyword title | MRR / recurring revenue positioning | Useful keyword model, but less brandable and more integration/revenue focused. |
| Simple Project Profit | Exact project-profit phrase | Track Costs & Profit Margins | Very close conceptually; validates "project profit" as a search/use-case phrase. |

Takeaway:

The niche apps usually use one of two naming structures:

- `Brand - Revenue Tracker`
- `Specific Keyword Tracker`

For this product, `IndieProfit` is stronger than a pure exact-match name because the audience is narrow and identity-based. The subtitle should carry the keyword weight.

### Broader Successful Adjacent Apps

| App | Current title pattern | Subtitle / visible promise | ASO lesson |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Spending Tracker | Simple generic keyword | Take Control Of Your Money | Mature apps can afford broad names because they have ratings/history. New apps cannot rely on this. |
| Expenses: Spending Tracker | Generic + keyword | Bill & Subscription Manager | Uses a broad title with a more specific subtitle to widen coverage. |
| Profit Tracker - Made Simple | Exact keyword + simplicity | All in one expense tracker | Strong example of repeating "profit", "expense", "tracker", "sales", and "small business" throughout the listing. |
| Cube Time & Expense Tracker | Functional keyword title | Track Clients, Projects, Tasks | Shows "time + expense + project" is a recognized B2B search pattern. |

Takeaway:

These apps prove that simple phrases such as `expense tracker`, `spending tracker`, `budget`, `income`, and `project` have high ASO value, but the first launch should not lead with them alone. They should be supporting keywords around a sharper indie-developer promise.

### 競品觀察繁體中文對照

| 競品類型 | 他們常用的 ASO 做法 | 對 IndieProfit 的啟發 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Indie / revenue tracker | 名稱直接放 `Revenue Tracker`、`MRR`、`Stripe`、`RevenueCat` 等明確詞 | 使用者意圖很清楚，但多數偏向已經有收入或串接服務的產品。IndieProfit 可以切入更早期的手動追蹤與回本判斷。 |
| Small business / profit tracker | 強調 sales、expenses、inventory、profit、export | 可借用 `profit`、`expense`、`income` 等詞，但不要變成 reseller 或小商家庫存工具的定位。 |
| Expense / budget tracker | 用非常寬的 `expense tracker`、`budget`、`spending` 搶搜尋量 | 新 App 很難正面競爭。這些詞適合放在 keyword field 或描述，不適合當主定位。 |
| Time & expense tracker | 強調 clients、projects、tasks、time、expenses | 證明「時間成本 + 專案成本」是成熟需求，但 IndieProfit 要改成 indie developer / app project 語境。 |

## Naming Recommendation

### Best Launch Name

App name:

`IndieProfit`

Subtitle:

`App Revenue & Break-even`

Why:

- `Indie` identifies the user.
- `Profit` identifies the desired outcome.
- The name is short enough to leave keyword work to the subtitle.
- It avoids sounding like personal finance, bookkeeping, or tax software.
- It fits both App Store and future Google Play title limits.

Risk:

- "Indie" is a niche word. It may reduce mass-market discovery but improve conversion from the right audience.
- Final availability still needs to be checked inside App Store Connect before submission. Public search did not show a direct App Store conflict during this research pass, but App Store Connect is the source of truth.

### Backup Names

| Name | Use if | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ProjectProfit | You want maximum keyword clarity | Generic and less brandable; similar wording exists in the market. |
| MakerLedger | You want a more premium maker/business feel | "Ledger" may feel too accounting-heavy. |
| BreakEven | You want to own the core feature | Too narrow and likely harder to protect as a brand. |
| ShipLedger | You want a product-building theme | Less obvious to users outside the shipping/building metaphor. |
| ProfitBoard | You want a dashboard-like brand | Broader, less indie-specific. |

### 命名建議繁體中文對照

| 名稱 | 中文解讀 | 優點 | 風險 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `IndieProfit` | 獨立開發者的獲利追蹤 | 最符合目標族群與產品結果，短、好記、可品牌化 | `Indie` 是小眾詞，會犧牲一部分大眾搜尋量 |
| `ProjectProfit` | 專案獲利追蹤 | 關鍵字非常直白 | 偏泛用商業工具，品牌感較弱，市場已有相近命名 |
| `MakerLedger` | 創作者帳本 / maker 財務紀錄 | 比較有質感，適合 maker 族群 | `Ledger` 可能太會計、太嚴肅 |
| `BreakEven` | 回本 / 損益平衡 | 核心功能很明確 | 範圍太窄，未來擴展到收入、時間成本、分享卡會受限 |
| `ShipLedger` | 產品發佈帳本 | 有 build / ship 的產品感 | 不熟 indie hacker 語境的使用者可能看不懂 |

首發建議仍然是 `IndieProfit`。它不像純 keyword name 那麼泛用，但更容易在小眾族群裡建立記憶點。

## iOS First Launch Metadata

Apple App Store limits currently relevant here:

- App name: up to 30 characters.
- Subtitle: up to 30 characters.
- Keyword field: 100 characters.
- Promotional text: useful for conversion and editable without a full binary update, but should not carry the core search strategy.

### Recommended English Variant

App Name:

`IndieProfit`

Subtitle:

`App Revenue & Break-even`

Keyword field:

`developer,mrr,expense,income,tracker,startup,founder,project,saas,side hustle,cost,roi,maker`

Promotional text:

`Track revenue, expenses, time cost, and break-even progress for every app, SaaS, or side project you are building.`

Description opening:

`IndieProfit helps indie developers see whether their apps and side projects are actually becoming profitable.`

Description:

`Track project revenue, expenses, time cost, and break-even progress in one focused dashboard. Instead of guessing whether a side project is working, see the numbers that matter: income, costs, net profit, hidden development cost, and how far each project is from break-even.`

`Built for indie developers, solo founders, SaaS builders, app makers, and creators managing multiple projects.`

Key features:

- Track income and expenses per project
- See net profit and loss
- Monitor break-even progress
- Track development hours and hidden time cost
- Use developer-focused categories like server, API, domain, AI tools, ads, design, and App Store fees
- Review monthly revenue and expense trends
- Celebrate milestones like first revenue, first profitable month, and reaching break-even
- Create shareable progress cards for build-in-public updates

Closing:

`IndieProfit is not a personal budget app. It is a project profitability tracker for people building real products.`

### More Search-Aggressive Variant

App Name:

`IndieProfit`

Subtitle:

`Revenue, Cost, Break-even`

Keyword field:

`developer,mrr,expense,income,tracker,startup,founder,project,saas,side hustle,profit,roi`

Tradeoff:

This variant covers more short search terms but reads less naturally than `App Revenue & Break-even`.

### More Conservative Variant

App Name:

`IndieProfit`

Subtitle:

`Project Profit Tracker`

Keyword field:

`developer,mrr,revenue,expense,income,startup,founder,saas,side hustle,break even,cost,roi`

Tradeoff:

This is clearer to users who do not know the app yet, but it overlaps more with generic small-business/project-profit apps.

## Screenshot ASO Strategy

The first 3 screenshots should carry the conversion argument. Use real product UI, not abstract illustrations.

1. `Is your app profitable yet?`
   Show revenue, expenses, net profit, and break-even progress on the project dashboard.

2. `Know when you break even`
   Show progress ring/bar, remaining amount, and recovery timeline.

3. `Track the cost of your time`
   Show logged hours, hourly rate, hidden cost, and effective return.

4. `Built for indie developers`
   Show categories like API, server, domain, App Store fee, AI tools, ads, and design.

5. `Share your progress`
   Show a build-in-public share card.

6. `Manage every side project`
   Show multiple app/SaaS projects in the project list.

## First Launch ASO Checklist

Before submission:

- Lock final name in App Store Connect as soon as possible.
- Confirm the exact app name is available in App Store Connect, not only public search.
- Use `IndieProfit` in onboarding, paywall, settings, screenshots, and privacy policy so the listing and app experience match.
- Avoid "best", "#1", "guaranteed", or unsupported income/profit claims.
- Keep the first sentence of the description clear enough to stand alone in previews.
- Make the screenshot captions match high-intent searches: revenue, expenses, break-even, time cost, side project.

After launch:

- Track product page views to installs.
- Track first-session activation: project created, first transaction added, break-even dashboard viewed.
- Track which keyword terms produce impressions through App Store Connect and Apple Search Ads Basic/Advanced data if available.
- After 2 to 4 weeks, test subtitle only if impressions are weak or conversion is clearly below expectations.

# SideProfit ASO Metadata

Date: 2026-05-21

Working app name: **SideProfit**

Assumption: `SideProfit` is the intended spelling. Do not use `SidePorfit` / `Sideporfit` in public metadata unless intentionally choosing a misspelled brand, which is not recommended.

## Positioning

Primary position:

> A project profit and break-even tracker for small apps, SaaS, side projects, and product experiments.

Core promise:

> Track whether a small project is becoming profitable.

Target users:

- Solo founders
- Indie developers
- Small product teams
- SaaS builders
- App makers
- Creators running paid side projects
- Anyone tracking whether a small project has paid for itself

Do not position primarily as:

- Personal finance
- Household budgeting
- Accounting
- Bookkeeping
- Tax software
- Client invoicing

Those terms can appear only as supporting search language where useful.

## App Store Limits Used

- App name: up to 30 characters.
- Subtitle: up to 30 characters.
- Keyword field: up to 100 bytes.
- Promotional text: up to 170 characters.
- Description: up to 4000 characters.

## Naming Risk Notes

`SideProfit` is broad enough to include non-developer small projects, but the word `Side` can imply side hustle or secondary income. That is acceptable if the product wants a broader market than indie developers, but screenshots and subtitle should keep the promise anchored around projects, revenue, expenses, and break-even progress.

If the product later narrows back to indie developers, `IndieProfit` is still stronger for identity-based positioning. If the product moves toward all small businesses, `ProjectProfit` is clearer but less brandable.
